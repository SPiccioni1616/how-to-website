Basic 5 Ingredient Cookie Recipe

Ingredients

    • 1 ½ cups brown sugar

    • 1 cup butter, softened

    • 2 large eggs

    • 3 cups all-purpose flour

    • 1 ½ cups semi-sweet chocolate chips




Directions


Step one

Gather the ingredients. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).


Step two

Beat sugar and butter together in a large bowl with an electric mixer until light and fluffier in texture. Beat in eggs, one at at time. Gradually stir in flour until well combined; fold in chocolate chips.


Step three

Spoon dough onto a baking sheet, keeping them about 2 inches apart. Bake in the preheated oven until edges are golden brown, 10 to 15 minutes.
